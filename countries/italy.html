About Italy at Eurovision

Italy was a founding member, left in 1997, and returned in 2011. Won in 1964, 1990, and 2021 (Måneskin, 'Zitti e buoni'). Big Five member. Fun fact: Italy influenced the 'Sanremo Music Festival' format that inspired Eurovision itself.

Why watch Italy in 2026
